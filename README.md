BreakFree is a website dedicated to helping individuals & families affected by drug abuse. It offers in-depth information, support & resources to help individuals take control of their thoughts, emotions & actions & to achieve lasting recovery.

## What It Is
      
BreakFree helps individuals & families overcome drug abuse & achieve lasting recovery. We understands the challenges & struggles that individuals & families face when dealing with drug abuse & BreakFree is here to provide support & guidance every step of the way.


## About

BreakFree provides a comprehensive approach to recovery that addresses both the physical & mental aspects of drug abuse. The goal is to help individuals & families understand the impact of drug abuse on their lives & to provide the tools & resources they need to take control of their thoughts, emotions & actions.
